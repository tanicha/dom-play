DOM Play

Uses JavaScript data attributes and a NodeList to highlight actors in a play by clicking on the name of the actors. (click on a name!)


Coffee Talk:

Freddy: Coffee, anyone? A buddy of mine gets it sent to him from Cuba and I must say that the coffee beans from there are impeccable. Such a smooth taste. Would you gentlemen like a cup of Cuba?

Tobey: No thanks.

Freddy: Don’t be rude. Introduce yourself first before answering me…this way I know the proper way to address you.

Tobey: Tobey.

Freddy: No coffee, Tobey?

Tobey: No, thank you.

John: What’s your name?

Freddy: My name? Excuse me, you can call me Freddy, Freddy Ferrara…

John: I’m John.

Freddy: Nice to me you, Johnny Boy. Coffee?

John: Sure.

Tobey: Actually, did you have any hazelnut coffee?

Freddy: Yes, I do. Wait here...

John: Thank you.

Tobey: Yeah, thank you so much!
